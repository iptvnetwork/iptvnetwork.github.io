## 🎯 User Flow

```
ENTRY
  ↓
[Home Page]
  • Browse channels
  • See 4-5 columns
  • See categories
  ↓
[Search/Filter]
  • Click category
  • Or search by name
  ↓
[Select Channel]
  • Click channel card
  • Opens player modal
  ↓
[Watch Video]
  • Player opens
  • Video plays
  • See controls
  • See related
  ↓
[Control Video]
  • Play/Pause
  • Volume
  • Fullscreen
  ↓
[Switch Channel]
  • Click related
  • Or close/browse
  ↓
[Exit]
  • Close modal
  • Back to browse
```
